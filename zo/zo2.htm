he western area of Changuha Coastal Industrial Park is Rouzongjiao beach. The view of the beach impresses visitors. When the sun sets, you can sit on the beach or armor unit to appreciate the sunset and miracle of the nature. Look around the beach, and you will love it!

Besides the sunset, why is fishing at Rouzongjiao beach so famous? This is because its special geographical environment. There are no “mad dog waves,” meaning suddenly big wave, here and the amount of fish is rich because the fish can hide themselves in the holes of the breakwater. One fisherman told us that someone made the record of catching more than thirty Black seabreams in one hour because of understanding the tide.

In fact, Ching-An Drain, nearby Wunzai Port, is also a natural place to fish. If you go along the seashore to the north of Changhua Coastal Industrial Park, you can find a place, named Neikuzi. It is another heaven of fishing.
If you come here on holidays, you will see people in crowds on the coast. One fisherman told us while fishing, he could smoke less, drink less and eat less. His wife even told him to live on the coast and not to come home. No wonder somebody said, “while fishing and raising birds, the wife will starve.” If you have the chances, you should come here to enjoy fishing and understand the feeling of catching a lot of fish.




A natural fishing place, attracting many fishermen

Although Hsien-Hsi beach is safer than other beaches, there are many people drowned at Rouzongjiao Beach every summer. Therefore, safe fishing is very important. Below is the safe fishing checklist.

  • Don’t fish alone, especially in the beginning of the fall. Because the wave is large, the accident probably happens. You had better fish with your friends. More people can help each other and kill the time through exchanging the experiences.
  • Remember to bring along the right attitude and the right stuff for safety, such as personal flotation device, a long-handle fish net, in case it is needed to reach out to someone in the water.
  • If you plan a night fishing, you had better come to the fishing place before it is dark. In addition, understanding the geography around the fishing place can keep you safer when the accident occurs. Avoid some dangerous places, such as the separated rock on the sea and the corner of stretching to the sea. The good fishing place is high because the wave is hard to roll up. In addition, some current is not obvious at Rouzongjiao Beach. Although it looks still, the danger is hide under the sea. Do not risk at a unfamiliar place.
  • Notice the weather. When the weather is bad, you had better leave. If you stay there, you will be in danger.
  • Be careful of the fish with sharp teeth, such as sea eels. Therefore, before you take the fish off the hook, you can cut the line first and wait the fish died, especially the fish with toxic thorns.
  • Never back onto the sea in any situation.
  • Look around for people and obstructions before you cast.
