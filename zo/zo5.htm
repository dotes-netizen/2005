It is extraordinary that many horse ranches are located at Rouzongjiao beach. When visitors walk on the beautiful beach, they can see people enjoy riding and trotting on the beach. Although the beach ranches mainly provide visitors a place for their vacation, horseback riding become the most popular event of the ranches. Besides beach ride, many outdoor activities are also hold every year, such as “Marriages on the Horses,” “Beach Ride Competition,” and “Cowboy & Horse.” In addition, kids can ride ponies in the pony arena and let their parents/guide guide them. When the parents guide their kids, their relationship will be improved. Therefore beach ride becomes a character of Hsien-Hsi beach. Besides beach ride, the businessmen also held beach motorcycle riding in irregular time. If visitors don’t want to ride the horse, they can make sand carving on the beach.


Above these activates were hold mainly by the Lin’s Horse Ranch, built in 1988. In the beginning, the Lin’s Horse Ranch was a beach ride club. It only provided the members a place to ride the horse and help them to raise the horses. Meanwhile, because horse race was in, horseback riding became popular in the center of Taiwan. At that time the club held many activities, such as horseback riding photography. It was very successful. However, many businessmen moved their industries to China and the horse price was high, the club turned into a entertainment ranch, White Horse Entertainment Ranch, and provided a tour for visitors, such as horseback riding, delicious food, healthy life, wine-brewing and preserved duck eggs making.
Now only three horses are left in the ranch. One is one and half year old “Peter;” another is six months pregnant “Little Nymphet;” the other is a pure arab. Owing to developing Changhua Coastal Industrial Park, the owner, who loves horse much, takes the horses to the beach only when the activates are hold or the visitors make a reservation. The owner’s wife said, “connecting the business, entertainment and the local culture is our goal.” If you want ride the horse, watch horse, make the preserved duck eggs, and taste wine, don’t forget to make a reservation to The Lin’s Horse Ranch.
