We only can catch some small crabs for fun in majority time at other beaches. One Chinese slang said, “earn a livelihood by the mountain while living in mountain; earn a livelihood by the sea while living at beach.” However, the situation is a little different here, because Hsien-Hsi beach is a sand beach. The tidal zone is wide. When the tide is down to the lowest on the first day or the fifteenth day in lunar month, the width of the tidal zone may extend to several hundred meters. Therefore, there are many organisms living here. Because of the poor land, the worse living environment, some local middle-age women and the older generation in Hsien–Hsi squatted to dig “Gmelins” or “Mud shrimp” in the sand beach before. They sold these animals with shells to the fish vendors. Not only they could earn some money, but also these two seafood became a specialty in Hsien-Hsi. Now if you visit here, you can try to take some simple tools to dig Gmelins or mud shrimp. Tuck your sleeve and pants. Let us try it in the beach!


“The red mouth young,” Gmelins, is the special species of shellfish in Taiwan. Therefore, it is also called “country saint clam.” Its volume is not big, and the round shell is thin, yellow brown and wheel line. The edge of the shell is purple ring, and the shell diameter is approximately 4 centimeters. The sausage vendor in Rouzongjiao Beach said, “ it was called the red mouth is because the edge of the shell is red and purple ring. You can see many people dig Gmelins at the beach near Ching-An drain.
Local people spend 6 hours to dig Gmelins and sell them to make money. They always use the time of the two ebbs everyday. The average amount is 3~6 kilograms each time. After digging, you can dig at the same place again when the tide recycle once. The residents said,“ one kilogram chosen Gmelins can be sold for 420 NT and one kilogram not chosen Gmelins is 250NT.
If you come here individually, you will certainly be lured by this activity. The family can trace the clam and concurrently wash you’re the trousers. In addition to enjoying the sea breeze and sand carving, you can take some Gmelins back if you catch the Gmelins yourself. It is really exciting. Therefore, before you come to this beach next time, you should check the tide schedule first and take several buckets, shovels with you. Don’t forget to bring one barrel clear water to wash your hands and feet. Then you will be able to enjoy digging Gmelins
After going home, you shall make the Gmelins spit the sand. You can fry the clam meat with Jioucengta, garlic, hot pepper and soy sauce, and the flavor is tasty. This is a good dish, especially mixing the soup and steam rice to eat. If the clams are caught by yourself, you will taste more delicious. So, try it next time.

Mud shrimp is the specialty of Lugang in Changhua. They are often installed into the small basket to peddle. The cooks like to fry them and add some pepper. While drinking, this dish is very suitable to eat. However, its origin is not from Lugang, but from Hsien-Hsi and Shen-Gang. In the early time, the cooking is put mud shrimp in the salt. It is extremely salty. Therefore, the residents spread one proverb “eating a mud shrimp can make you eat three bowls of gruel.” Although the mud shrimp is the food of the poor people, it becomes very expensive because it is not easy to dig today. So, when we go to dig mud shrimp in Rouzongjaio Beach, we will understand the ecology of the mud shrimp and have much fun.

Instruction to Mud Shrimp
The systematic name of mud shrimp is Upogebia edulis Ngoc-Ho and Chan. It is one kind of crabs. They mainly live beneath the mud flats at sand beach. They usually undermine a long tunnel and live in the sand. When the tide is in, they will crawl along the tunnel to the hole in the neighborhood for grasping the under-water little creature and food pellet to eat. When the tide is down, they will hide in the deep cavern, which can extend to half meter deep. The swimming ability of the mud shrimp is extremely poor so they hide in the sand for a long time. The sand will protect them. Both the strong current and the mobile sand are not suitable for them to live. Therefore, they usually hide in the half-meter deep sand.

Digging and Gather Mud Shrimp together
After you find the suitable place, you might look over the beach. If you see many small trucks, called steel ox cars, stop on the beach and hear the sound of the motor pumping the water, that will likely be that the fishermen are digging mud shrimp. You can be ready to join them and learn something about digging mud shrimp.
If you use the shovels to dig mud shrimp, you will fail. An experienced fisherman will carry one pump, a long water pipe and a hard plastic pipe. He will connect all of them and stick the hard plastic pipe into the sand, and then the mud shrimp will be flush upon to the surface of the sand by the strong current. As long as the mud shrimp is flush to the ground, it will be caught because of its slow movement.
However, how do the fishermen know where the mud shrimp hide? This will depend on the experiences and wise of the fishermen. The holes of mud shrimp are harder because they have to crawl upon to feed. The holes are smaller than those of the crabs. Therefore, as long as this kind of hole get more, the fishermen will drive their small truck to catch mud shrimp. You can observe this situation, and it must surprise you.
While flushing the mud shrimp, there are many other creatures flushed out. Therefore, you also can use these chances to observe the little animals living in sand beach.

How to catch mud shrimp
If you want to exercise, you can try to catch mud shrimp. First, you can choose a place, which a lot of mud shrimps live in and the area is about 4 square meters. Then you can use the shovel to dig, and put the dig mud together around the hole. In the beginning of digging, the water will seep out in the middle. You have to dig and then step. The depth has to reach half meter. The function of stepping is to mix the mud and seawater. After so for a half-hour, a bid muddy hole is formed and the mud shrimp cannot stand the muddy environment as to crawl out. Now you can catch them easily. Someone once caught about 2 kilograms mud shrimp. The amount was surprising. This is the simple method to catch mud shrimp. Do it with you whole family. It must make your family have much fun and go on a diet. After your exercise, remember to restore the original condition.
When we dig the mud shrimp, why do the mud shrimps come out? This is because they have to breathe with the branchia like the other shrimps and crabs. As long as the water is dirty, they will not breathe well. Therefore, they will crawl up to search the clean water. However, after digging mud shrimps, remember to restore the original condition.

The tools for digging mud shrimp
