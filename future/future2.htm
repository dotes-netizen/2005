The thoughts of the visitors on Wunzai Port
We observed Wunzai Port with a view of local people. To understand the thoughts of the visitors, we designed questionnaires to interview the visitors. According to their thoughts, we will modify our program and give Wunzai Port a way to the port with local culture. Below are our questionnaires and results.
　　


Dear visitors:
We are the junior high school students who are doing our research about Hsien-Hsi Beach. To understand the thoughts of the visitors on Wunzai Port, below is the questionnaire. Thank you for your help.
Hsien-His junior high school students, 2005/2/15

(1) Where are you from?

(2) Ages?

three times a week 0%, once a week 0%, once every two weeks 0%, once a month 36%, several times a year 64%
 
3. The geography & designation of Wunzai Port

(5) Which part of Wunzai Port do you think have to improve?

(6) Do you have any suggestion or thought?

b. Add more advertisements and the maps of the attractives

d. Add the areas of the port through building the bridge between the bank and Changhua Coastal Industrial Park.

6. The others
(1) Do you think what are the characters of Wunzai Port? Which parts are different from the other ports?
a. The scale is small and the equipments are poor.
b. The water is not deep. It is a small township port.
c. With a traditional fish market
Most visitors feel it is a little pity that Wunzai Port is next to Changhua Coastal Industrial Park. However, because the scale is not big, it is hard to compete with the other ports. If Wunzai Port can develop its characters, it will have a good future.
(2) Do you like Wunzai Port? How do you think about Wunzai Port?
Many visitors like Wunzai Port much. They think this port is very simple and the view is very beautiful especially looking over the sea.
(3) What do you think the souvenirs are?
Visitors all think the most special character of Wunzai Port is they can choose any fresh seafood by themselves. Another visitor said that the roast cuttlefish is too delicious to refuge it. He always buys two stick roast cuttlefish at Wunzai Port each time.
