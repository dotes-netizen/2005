The Prospect of Hsien-Hsi Beach Local People Visitors Wild Bird Association Interviewing the Head of Township

After reading our introduction, is Hsien-Hsi beach worth visiting? Just like our topic “ An ignored Pearl on the Sea,” although Hsien-Hsi has a natural beautiful beach and many geographical types of scenery, it is still not a famous port.
There are many reasons to limit the development of Hsien-Hsi. The first one is Hsien-Hsi is a traditional township, and because of the change of economic structures and the moving out of the population, the government want to develop Hsien-Hsi through establishing Changhua Coastal Industrial Park. Therefore, the environment is getting damage. In addition, because the local people farm oyster along the seashore, the habitats of migratory birds are threatened. Finally, owing to no good plans and traffic, Hsien-Hsi still cannot become an ideal attractive. Thereupon, we try to give Hsien-Hsi a unique and brand new future through collecting the information and interviewing the local people, visitors and the head of the township.
