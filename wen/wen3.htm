Yuancyuan Seafood Restaurant
Owner: Yuan-Cyuan Huang
Address: No.173 1st Section coastal Rd Yu-Pu village Hsien-Hsi Town
Phone Number: 7581833, 7580613,7987385
Opening Hour: AM10:00-PM2: 30, PM5:00-PM9:00
Introduction:
A big tank is what you can see when you enter the restaurant. There are ells, clams, raninidaes, razor clams, mud shrimps in it, and these are the important ingredients for cooking. All the seafood is fresh and there isn’t actually a menu in the store. Customers choose the seafood that they want to eat and they can ask to cook them directly. The special dish in this restaurant is the steamed trout. Ells and shark fins are popular, too.

Eels are eaten in two ways, one is in soup and the other is in three cups of seasoning including sugar, soy sauce and sesame oil. It’s simple to make ell soup. What you need to do is to cut eels into pieces, and boil them with water. It’s more complicated to do it in the way of three cups. First, fry the eel to crispen it. Then, put ginger, garlic, soy sauce, sugar, sesame oil, plum wine into the soup and boil for half an hour.

Mr. Huang said other restaurants cook eels with the Shaosing wine, which makes the flavor sour. However, he uses plum wine as substitute, and it makes the soup sweet.

There are also two ways to cook shark fins, with soup and the fragrant flower. The first step to cook shark fin soup is to put then in water for a while, and then fry them with dried bamboo shoots, parsley, garlic, and egg. Add some sesame oil as seasoning. All steps above make a wonderful dish.

Sincinming Seafood Booth
Owner: Cin-Ming Wu
Address: No.908 1st Sec. coastal Rd. Yu-Pu village Hsien-Hsi Town
Phone Number: 7581847
Introduction:
What you will see when entering the restaurant is a display rack. There is seafood on it, like clams, shrimps, and squids. Among these, the most popular are squid balls. The owner says most of the seafood comes from the southern port of Taiwan, only a few of it form Wunazi Port.

The squid balls contain much real squid meat. First, you have to chop the squids, and make it into a ball shape. Steam and fry them with some flour. They really taste good.

Heiren Seafood Restaurant
Owner: Jhih-Sin Shin
Address: No.758 1st Section coastal Rd Hsien-Hsi Town
Phone Number: 7587499 or 7587608
Opening Hour: AM10:00-PM3:00 PM5:00-PM10:00
Introduction:
The owner of Heiren Seafood Restaurant has a daughter who is on the second grade of our school. The owner’s hospitality and the open karaoke make the restaurant characteristic. The hostess says customers are used to drinking while eating seafood. After they are drunk, singing can’t be avoided.

The main courses in the store are all about fish and shrimp. Customers are both from local residents and visitors. In the beginning, most customers were from Chang-Hau Coastal Industrial Park, but after SARS, fewer and fewer people come. They like to order fried tree fresh fish (squids, shrimps, sea cucumbers), sweet and sour mullet, and shrimp ball with pineapple most. In winter, mullet roe and mullet’s swim bladder are available. These are the special dieses in this restaurant.

Siangwei Seafood Restaurant
Owner: Wun-Sian Lin
Address: No.930 coastal Rd Yu-Pu village Hsien-Hsi Town
Phone Number: 7581761or 7582248
Introduction:
When you walk in Sianwei Seafood Restaurant, you will feel the hospitality from the owner. Besides, there is a big parking lot for customers. Mr. Lin says most the seafood in the restaurant is from Wunzai Pork. However, the species here are not enough, so he import some form Pun-Hu, Kin-Man, and Mainland China.

The restaurant has been opened for six years and customers mainly are from the workers in Chang-Hau Coastal Industrial Park, and some visitors, too. They often come here from 12 to 1 o’clock for lunch, and 6:30 to 8:30 in the evening for dinner. During Chinese New Year, the business is even better. All the dishes are done directly after customers’ order. The owner says when it comes to run the business, hygiene, service, and freshness are basic points he has to insist.

There are many different kinds of seafood in the restaurant, such as eels, lobsters, , mud shrimps, crab, virgin crab, porgy and blowfish. The owner will teach you some knowledge about fish when you are ordering. Mr. Lin tells us there are two kinds of crabs. Those without copulation are called virgin crabs, and others are called crabs. Besides, blowfishes contain many species. One kind is toxic and another one isn’t. Some are with thorns and others aren’t. The owner especially introduces the specialty eels, which can be cooked into boxthorn eel soup, eel salad, and sweet and sour eels.
