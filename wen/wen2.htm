About Wunzai Port Port Scenery Fresh Seafood Seafood Booths & Restaurants

There are a hallway, built with steel, and many vendors in rows in Wunzai Port. The owner of each dragger almost has one booth to sell his fish. Because they sell and buy directly, the price is very cheap. In addition, the variety of fresh seafood is rich, such as gymnura, dory, big scat, sand shrimp and warsaw grouper. Furthermore, the famous mud shrimps in Lugang are also sold here.

Before Wunzai port locates a seafood market, selling all kinds of fresh seafood. Visitors can choose by themselves. The fishermen sail to the sea at about 2 or 3 o’clock in the morning and come back at about 5 o’clock. It is also the best time to buy the fresh fish when the draggers come back. Many fish businessmen also come here to purchase a lot of fresh seafood at this moment. On the other hand, visitors will crush into this small market from 3 o’clock to 6 o’clock in the afternoon. In addition, many visitors come here to buy fish on holidays, and then the vendors will laugh loudly and happily. Until the sky get dark, the visitors leave, and the booths close.
Although this port is not famous in Taiwan, it is the best one for the people who live nearby Hsien-Hsi.
