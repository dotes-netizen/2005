About Wunzai Port Port Scenery Fresh Seafood Seafood Booths & Restaurants

While following the driving directions to enter Hsien-Hsi beach and exiting at West Coastal Freeway, visitors will see “Welcome to Wunzai Port” on the bridge. If they drive right, they will arrive at the seafood market, seafood restaurant and the pier; if they drive left, they will get to a parking lot and seafood building.

Visitors can see many draggers stop at the pier of Wunzai Port. Because local fishermen depend on these draggers to earn a livelihood, these draggers will be tied at the pier before sailing to the sea. When the fishermen prepare to sail to the sea, we can see the expectation on their face. Each time the draggers come back, full of fish, the seafood market always become very noisy. The seafood booths in rows show all kinds of fresh seafood. Visitors can hear the vendors’ yelling and the other visitors’ voice of surprise. If you want to immediately taste the fish, which you buy, you can ask the seafood restaurant to cook for you. On the weekend, the vendors and the visitors are in crowds beside the road.
For changing Wunzai Port into a port for sightseeing, the local government built the new viewing platform. When visitors stand on the platform, they can enjoy the gentle breeze and look over the sea. Therefore, they will be lured by the view of the beautiful port.
